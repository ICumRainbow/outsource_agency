Advantages of Employing a Committed Development Team

01

Agile Development

This refers to a methodology for software development that emphasizes iterative and incremental delivery of the final product, with a focus on adaptability and customer satisfaction.

02

Project Management

The implementation of modern methods into your organizational procedures will be facilitated by our team of Project Managers.

03

Efficient Scalability & Talent Oversight

You can concentrate on your product while we take care of the rest - you don't need to be an HR firm. Our services encompass end-to-end recruitment and employee satisfaction processes.

04

Acquiring digital products that are already in existence.

Our engineers are knowledgeable in undertaking projects burdened with technical debt. Our process begins by conducting a thorough review of your existing codebase, after which we propose appropriate solutions such as updates, migration, and so on.

What is our process for assembling a specialized team?

01

Exploratory workshop

The purpose is to gain insight into your business objectives and client requirements, thereby enabling us to identify the most suitable team composition to deliver on those goals.

02

Task distribution

As a team, we collaborate to establish comprehensive job requirements for potential team members and identify the necessary resources needed to effectively manage the team.

03

Initial screening of candidates.

You'll be provided with a roster of candidates and their resumes who possess the relevant skills, experiences, and firsthand knowledge to meet your project's specific requirements and expectations.

04

Interviews

After you have made your selection, each candidate can proceed with an interview conducted by either your HR or Tech Team to assess whether they meet your criteria.

05

Experience adoption

We also offer the option to carry out this process at your office. We highly recommend that our Dedicated Team gets to know your employees and familiarizes themselves with your company's culture.

06

Daily status reports

Dedicated Team is seamlessly integrated into your own team, and provides daily progress reports on their activities, as well as participating in regular stand-up calls and other necessary meetings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "dedicated software development team" mean?

A Dedicated Software Development Team refers to a group of chosen software developers who are solely dedicated to developing your software solution. They are an external team, typically based offshore or nearshore.

How we build our management process for dedicated teams?

The management of a Dedicated Software Development Team can either be handled by your own organization or delegated to the project manager from the company that you have contracted the team from.

What are the benefits of outsourcing a dedicated software development team?

Outsourcing a dedicated software development team can bring several benefits, such as access to a larger talent pool, cost savings, increased efficiency and flexibility, and the ability to focus on your core business functions while leaving the technical development to the experts. Additionally, outsourcing allows you to scale your team up or down as needed, depending on the project's requirements.
