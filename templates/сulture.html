Creating Experiences

Since 2013 we have been providing global headquarters with exceptional digital solutions, from mobile apps to complex infrastructure.

Nowadays, Q is recognized for creating experiences across countries while challenging boundaries across industries and exceeding users’ expectations.

We bring our services to our clients’ doorstep with five global offices in Zagreb, London, Belgrade, Belfast, New York, and Zürich. This approach led us to long-term relationships resulting in many award-winning products.

Our core (Q) values support the company vision and shape its culture.

01

Move Fast.

We constantly educate ourselves about market and tech trends to stay ahead of the curve and understand user needs regarding specific problems.

02

Be Kind.

In Q, we keep our word and never break promises. We value others, always look for win-win scenarios, and put ourselves in the shoes of others. Whatever the situation, we are candid, appreciative of other people’s views, and make time to help and support each other.

03

Do more.

Going the extra mile is what we always do in our work. We take the initiative, deliver more than expected, and push our boundaries each day.
