How Do You Build Your Dedicated Team?

01

Discovery workshop

The goal of the workshop is to understand your business goals and your clients’ needs in order to know the type of team that will achieve them.

02

Job specification

Together, we define detailed job specifications for each team candidate, as well as all the resources that you require for the management of the team.

03

Candidates preselection

You will receive a list of candidates and their resumés. They possess specific skills, experiences and first-hand knowledge that match your project requirements and expectations.

04

Interviews

Each candidate you(?) select can go through the interview with your HR or Tech Team to see if they meet your requirements.

05

Onboarding

This process can be done at your office too. We encourage our clients to have the Dedicated Team meet your employees as well as learn about your company’s culture.

06

Daily reporting

Dedicated team becomes an integral part of your team which includes daily reports on what they’ve been working on, standup calls etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a dedicated software development team?

A dedicated software development team is a team of selected programmers who will be working exclusively on your software solution. They are an outsourced team, either nearshore or offshore.

How is a dedicated team managed?

A team of dedicated software developers can be managed either by your company or by the project manager from the company that you hired the team from.

What are the most trusted countries where expert, dedicated development teams can be hired?

Eastern European countries are among the top destinations for hiring dedicated software teams. You can see that If you look at platforms like Clutch.
